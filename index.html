Strategic Focus for 180+ Marks

A 180-mark target is achievable with a smart strategy. It's not about studying everything, but studying the right things well.

Phase 1: High-Priority Chapters

Master the top 50-60% of the syllabus shown in the charts. Aim for 100% accuracy in these "must-do" chapters from all three subjects.

Phase 2: Mock Tests & Analysis

Start taking full-length mock tests early. Your goal is to consistently score 180-200. Analyze mistakes to identify and fix weak areas.

Phase 3: Medium & Easy Chapters

Secure easy marks from "low-weightage" but simple chapters (e.g., Mathematical Reasoning, EM Waves). These are your rank boosters.
